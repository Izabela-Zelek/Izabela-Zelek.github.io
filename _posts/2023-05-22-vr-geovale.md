Geo Vale is a **VR** farming simulation developed in **Unity** that immerses players in a tranquil, open-ended village life. The player takes on the role of a farmer responsible for cultivating crops, managing resources, and supplying produce to local shopkeepers within a living world driven by dynamic NPC schedules, day–night cycles, and environmental systems. Core gameplay revolves around planting, watering, harvesting, and selling crops, alongside auxiliary activities such as tree cutting, animal care, and basic resource management through a compact inventory system. A key feature is the in-game level editor, allowing players to freely shape the environment by placing and modifying objects, spawning NPCs, and creating custom navigation paths to influence world behaviour.

Built with Unity, Geo Vale uses **low-poly** rendering with level-of-detail optimisation, physics-based interaction, and 3D audio to maintain performance and immersion. Designed as a non-competitive, single-player experience with no defined victory conditions, the project focuses on relaxation, player creativity, and systemic world simulation within a cohesive rural sandbox environment.
